### Term Explanation

The Panoptic Incentive Points (Pips) Program aims to incentivize user participation, bootstrap liquidity, and encourage the adoption of DeFi options. Users engaged in various activities within the Panoptic ecosystem can earn Pips, rewarding them for their engagement and contribution. Details [here](https://pips.panoptic.xyz).
