# Panoptic — Company Profile

## Product
Panoptic is the first DeFi-native perpetual options protocol. It enables permissionless trading of perpetual put and call options on any ERC-20 token, powered by Uniswap v3/v4 liquidity. No expiries, no oracles, no intermediaries.

## Core Features
- **Perpetual Options** — No expiry dates; hold positions indefinitely
- **Any Token** — Create options markets on any asset with a Uniswap pool
- **Up to 10x Leverage** — Capital-efficient trading with built-in risk controls
- **Multi-leg Strategies** — Combine puts, calls, and spreads in a single position
- **Oracle-Free Pricing** — Derived from Uniswap liquidity, not external oracles
- **Self-Custody** — Fully non-custodial; your keys, your funds
- **Passive Liquidity Provision** — Earn yield by lending capital to options traders
