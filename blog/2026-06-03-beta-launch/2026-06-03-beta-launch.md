## Overview
Over the past months, Panoptic Prime members have been helping us test, refine, and shape the future of the protocol through our private beta program. We want to thank every community member who has participated, submitted feedback, reported bugs, and helped us push the platform forward.
The beta provides early access to new features, product improvements, and upcoming [vault strategies](/docs/getting-started/vaults) ahead of the broader [Panoptic V2](/blog/panoptic-v2-is-coming) release.
### What’s New?
-   **Vault Strategies:** Designed to simplify access to options-based strategies through automated vault products.
-   **A New Options Trading Interface:** Making discovering, opening, and managing positions more intuitive for traders.
-   **Request-for-Quote (RFQ) Functionality:** Enabling users to access liquidity and improved execution for options trades.
-   **Integrated Lending and Borrowing Markets:** Allowing users to put idle capital to work while providing the liquidity needed to support trading activity across the protocol.
## Who Participates
Access to the beta program is currently limited to members of Panoptic Prime, our community of traders, liquidity providers, and ecosystem participants. This community lives primarily in our Discord, so if you’d like to [join](https://discord.com/invite/8sX5Af2KXG). Prime members play an important role in helping shape the future of Panoptic by providing direct feedback.
## Why a Private Beta?

Building options infrastructure requires a high degree of reliability and confidence. Before releasing new features to the broader public, we use the beta program to:
-   Validate user experience and product design
-   Identify bugs and edge cases
-   Gather qualitative feedback from active users
-   Test new workflows in real market conditions
This process allows us to improve product quality while ensuring new releases meet the standards expected by our users.
